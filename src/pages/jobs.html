What kind of career can a CS major have?

CS careers range from building mobile apps to defending networks, analyzing data, and creating intelligent systems. You can mix technical depth with people‑focused work like product management or UX engineering.

Software Engineer

Design, build, and maintain applications and services (web, mobile, backend).

Data/ML Engineer

Build data pipelines and machine learning systems used in analytics and AI products.

Cybersecurity Analyst

Protect systems, respond to incidents, and improve security posture.

Cloud/DevOps Engineer

Automate infrastructure, CI/CD, and reliability at scale.

Product Manager

Define problems, coordinate teams, and ship features customers love.

UX Engineer

Bridge design and engineering to build delightful interfaces.

Note: Many students start broadly as software engineers, then specialize after exposure to different projects.

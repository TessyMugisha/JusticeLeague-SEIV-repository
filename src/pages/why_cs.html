Why should I be a Computer Science major?

CS sits at the center of innovation—powering apps, AI, cybersecurity, health tech, fintech, and more. It offers creative problem-solving, high demand globally, and the chance to build tools that help people at scale.

Reasons students choose CS:

  • Impact: Build products used by thousands or millions.
  • Flexibility: Work in any industry—tech, health, finance, sports, government.
  • Creativity: Turn ideas into working software quickly.
  • Growth: Skills stay relevant as technology evolves.
  • Portability: Remote-friendly roles and global opportunities.

CS isn’t only about coding—it’s about thinking clearly, modeling complex problems, and communicating solutions. If you enjoy puzzles, building things, and learning by doing, CS could be a great fit.

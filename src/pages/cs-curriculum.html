Typical CS Curriculum

Exact courses vary by school, but most programs follow a similar path from fundamentals to systems and electives.

Year 1

Intro to CS, Programming I/II, Discrete Math, Ethics in Tech.

Year 2

Data Structures, Computer Architecture, Probability/Stats, Web Dev.

Year 3

Algorithms, Operating Systems, Databases, Networks.

Year 4

Security, AI/ML, Distributed Systems, Capstone Project, Electives.
